For Students

To have an app that can show him the bus schedule and any delays or changes, so he can plan his route and avoid waiting in the heat or rain.

For Parents

To have an app that can show him all his children's buses in one place, so he can coordinate their schedules.

For Admin/Staff

A school/college bus tracking app that allows her to manage the bus service efficiently, ensure the safety of all student who use the school/college bus service and provide the best service possible to parents and students.
